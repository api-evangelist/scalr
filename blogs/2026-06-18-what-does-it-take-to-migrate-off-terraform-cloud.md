Migrating off Terraform Cloud typically takes days rather than months, with one 1,000-workspace estate migrated in roughly 2 hours after a pilot week and cutover downtime around 20 minutes. Migration tools from Scalr, Spacelift, and env0 automate most tasks, while sensitive variables and Sentinel policy rewrites stay manual; effort scales with variable count and policy complexity rather than workspace quantity.
